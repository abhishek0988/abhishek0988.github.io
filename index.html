Abhishek Agrawal

Hi! I'm Abhishek and this is a collection of some of my art and engineering projects.

I'm currently living in San Francisco and working on a new company with Ryan Noon and Chris Park.

Previously, I was an early PM at Dropbox and an an engineer at a Microsoft Incubation Lab.

I studied at Princeton (Electrical Engineering) and Harvard (MBA).

Projects

  • Dropbox Recents
  • Dropbox Full-text Search, an instant indexing system built fully in-house
  • Physical Dropbox, another DIY 3D Scanner made with a line laser
  • TrivPals, a turn based trivia game for iOS
  • Human Antenna, transmitting signals wirelessly via the capacitance of the skin
  • A DIY 3D Scanner made with a webcam, rescued turntable and an LED matrix
  • Ribbon Hero, a game to teach kids Microsoft Office
  • Chart Advisor, automated data visualization in Excel
  • Chemical sensing with quantum cascade lasers (CLEO 2007)
